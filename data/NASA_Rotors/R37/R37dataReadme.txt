Aerodynamic survey data - overall performance and radial surveys at Stn 3,4

notes : 
	tangential velocity is normalized by tip speed, Utip=454.14 m/sec
	temperature ratio is t4/tstd, where tstd=std day temperature
	pressure ratio is p4/pstd, where pstd= std day pressure
	efficiency is adiabatic efficiency
	

File format:

filename
NPTS = number of data points in the file
X    = NPTS x-values of data
Y    = NPTS y-values of data

Some of the file names are listed below:
.prb signifies a probe measurement
.lfa signifies an LDA measurement
Z4 signifies STN4 downstream of the rotor
Z3 signifies STN3 downstream of the rotor 

angz3_92.lfa	flow angle vs span, Stn 3, m/mchoke=0.92, LDA measurement
pr_all.prb	overall pressure ratio vs massflow, probe measurement
tr_all.prb	overall temperature ratio vs massflow
eff_all.prb	overall adiabatic efficiency vs massflow
prz4_92.prb	stn 4 pressure ratio, m/mchoke=0.92
prz4_98.prb	stn 4 pressure ratio, m/mchoke=0.98
trz4_92.prb	stn 4 temperture ratio, m/mchoke=0.92, thermocouple measurment
trz4_98.prb	stn 4 temperature ratio, m/mchoke=0.98, 	"	"
